I obtained a Ph.D. degree in the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong (CityU) advised by Prof. HOU Junhui. The qualifying panel members are Prof. WONG Hau San and Prof. CHAN Antoni Bert.

I am an Innovation and Technology Fund (ITF)-funded Postdoctoral Fellow in electrical engineering at the Chinese University of Hong Kong. My research passion lies at the intersection of brain data analysis, computer vision, and machine learning. I strive to develop innovative and intelligent systems that empower seamless human-machine interaction and collaboration while fostering trustworthy clinical decision-making. My current focus is self-supervised embedding learning, graph structure learning, and multi-agent modeling.
